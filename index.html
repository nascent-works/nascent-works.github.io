About Nascent

Nascent is a technology investor with a focus on experimental digital products. Our key areas of interest include AI solutions (Computer Vision, object recognition), cloud services, e sports, gaming and mobile applications.

We also foster relationships with early-stage startups and entrepreneurs and act as technology investor to bring great ideas into life. Nascent is headquartered in New York, USA, with R&D office in Sunrise Valley Tech Park, Vilnius, Lithuania.
