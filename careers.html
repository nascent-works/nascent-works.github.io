Applying

Please send your application to career@nascent-works.com.

By sending my resume to career@nascent-works.com I do agree to share my personal data that will be used and stored the following:

Purposes for which we process your personal data

Your data will be processed exclusively for the purposes of completing the application process. You can withdraw your application and the right to process your data that you have given us at any time (via email to career@nascent-works.com). By submitting an application, you are making your data available to the HR department. Your application documents may be viewed and checked by people involved in the Nascent-works selection process in connection with the filling of a position.

Storage duration and deletion of data

The data will be stored for as long as you are involved in the application process. If your application for employment is unsuccessful, we may hold your data on file for 6 months after the end of the relevant recruitment process in order to consider you for new positions, as well as for system administration, to perform research and analysis.
